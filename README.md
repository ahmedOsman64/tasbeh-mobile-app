# tasbeh-mobile-app
A simple, user-friendly digital tasbeeh counter app designed to help you keep track of your daily dhikr (Zikr) and tasbihat anytime, anywhere. This app provides a peaceful, distraction-free experience to strengthen your spiritual practice and connection with Allah (SWT).
Key Features:
Automatic Save: Your dhikr count is saved automatically, even if you close the app, so you can resume exactly where you left off.
Vibration & Sound Alerts: Receive optional subtle vibration or sound feedback with each tap, so you can focus on your recitation without needing to look at the screen.
Customizable Dhikr: Easily add and manage a list of your specific adhkar, wazaif, or common tasbihat like SubhanAllah, Alhamdulillah, and Allahu Akbar.
